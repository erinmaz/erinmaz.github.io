We are currently recruiting a fully-funded (minimum $27K/year stipend) MSc student. Details about the project and applying are here.  

Erin is eligible to supervise master’s students in Computer Science at StFX, Biology at StFX, as well as co-supervise graduate students (comp projects, master’s, and PhD) in Psychology and Neuroscience at Dalhousie University. Please contact Erin to discuss possibilities.

Prospective Honours Students

Erin is eligible to supervise StFX honours students in Psychology, Computer Science, Biology, and Health.

For MRI-based honours projects, students should have some background in neuroscience, computer science, or medical imaging. Suggested courses at StFX include:

  • CSCI 161 Introduction to Programming 
  • PHYS 250 Medical Imaging
  • PSYC 231/232 Brain and Behaviour

In addition, StFX students wishing to be considered for an honours position in the Mazerolle Lab should plan to take PSYC 394 (Advanced Statistics) in their third year and apply for research funding for the summer before their honours year.
