About

 

We use advanced brain imaging techniques to understand the interactions between brain function and blood supply in the healthy brain and neurological disease.

Ongoing projects in the lab also include the development and evaluation of neuroscience knowledge translation activities and using freely-available databases to attempt to replicate existing brain imaging studies.
